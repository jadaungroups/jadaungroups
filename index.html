About Us

Jadaun Groups is a Rajasthan-based family-owned business committed to delivering pure, authentic, and premium-quality Indian spices to households and food businesses across India.

Founded by Shri Udaibhan Singh Jadaun, the company was built with a strong vision to provide hygienic, lab-tested, and chemical-free spices that maintain the true essence of Indian taste and tradition. The inspiration behind Jadaun Groups came from a real concern about the growing adulteration in the spice market. Many spices available today contain artificial colours, low-quality ingredients, and harmful mixing practices. Shri Udaibhan Singh Jadaun believed that Indian families deserve spices that are as pure and high quality as the products available in international markets. With this vision, he started the journey of creating trusted masalas made with honesty, purity, and traditional values. Today, Jadaun Groups proudly operates through its trusted brands Rajput Spices and Rajbhog Spices. Every product is carefully selected, hygienically processed, and packed under strict quality standards to ensure natural aroma, authentic flavour, and rich freshness. The company serves retailers, wholesalers, distributors, hotels, restaurants, and food businesses with reliable bulk spice supply across India. The business is proudly supported by the next generation of the Jadaun family. Bhupesh Singh Jadaun plays an important role in managing and expanding the business operations, while Yuvraj Singh Jadaun focuses on building the company’s modern digital presence, branding, and future growth vision. Together, the family is working towards transforming Jadaun Groups into a trusted national and international spice brand while preserving the traditional values on which the company was founded. At Jadaun Groups, we strongly believe that good food begins with pure ingredients. Our mission is to provide every customer with safe, hygienic, and authentic Indian spices that enhance both taste and health. We remain committed to quality, trust, and customer satisfaction in every product we deliver. “Accha Khao, Accha Khilao.”
