About Us

Jadaun Groups is a Rajasthan-based family-owned spice business founded by Shri Udaibhan Singh Jadaun with a vision to provide pure, hygienic, and authentic Indian spices to every household and food business across India.

The company was started after realizing that many spices available in the market contain harmful adulteration, artificial colours, and low-quality ingredients that can negatively affect people’s health and lead to several diseases.

Shri Udaibhan Singh Jadaun believed that Indian families deserve spices that are not only rich in taste but also safe, natural, and trustworthy. His dream was to fight against the growing problem of impure and chemically mixed masalas by creating products made with honesty, quality, and traditional values.

Inspired by the premium-quality spices available in foreign countries, he wanted to introduce the same level of purity, hygiene, and international-standard quality in India while preserving the authentic flavour of Indian cuisine.

Today, Jadaun Groups proudly operates through its trusted brands Rajput Spices and Rajbhog Spices. Every product is carefully selected, hygienically processed, and lab-tested to maintain freshness, aroma, natural colour, and authentic taste without unnecessary artificial additives.

The business is strongly supported by the next generation of the Jadaun family. Bhupesh Singh Jadaun and Yuvraj Singh Jadaun they have the future vision of expanding the company across India and international markets.

At Jadaun Groups, our mission is simple — to provide pure and healthy spices that improve both taste and well-being while building a trusted Indian brand known for quality, honesty, and customer satisfaction.
